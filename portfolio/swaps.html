Swaps

Service Exchange Application

Swaps is a service exchange application that connects users through providing and consuming services by online tracking and paying.

Service providers are limited to unreliable sites such as Craigslist to post their services with bogged down posts and a lack of administration, providers and seekers of service do not have an efficient way of connecting. The solution was to create an interface experience that will allow people to offer services and consumers to take advantage of that service. The site will keep track of the services offered and allow users to pay online or “swap” services they provide.
