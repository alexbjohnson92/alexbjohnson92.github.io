Living Canvas

Experience Design

Living Canvas exposes the art form and culture of tattoos through an interactive facility and web experience, connecting and engaging tattoo enthusiasts, tattoo artists, and the general public.

Tattoo enthusiasts are not connected to tattoo culture or community around the world and are limited to their hometown tattoo shop, limiting their knowledge and experience of the art. This creates a negative perspective and connotation on the art form of tattoos. Our solution was exposing and connecting the public to the true art form, history, culture, and lifestyle of tattoos through a unique engaging experience.
