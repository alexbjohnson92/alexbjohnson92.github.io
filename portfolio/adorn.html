Adorn

Event Planning & Design

Adorn is an annual holiday party hosted by Multiple, Inc. to bring family, friends, and clients together to mingle, raise money for Greater Chicago Food Depository, and celebrate the holiday season.

The objective of Adorn is to raise money and collect parishables for those in need. Our solution was to design themed graphics to create awareness about the party and to raise money, we asked everyone to create their own homemade ornament to bring to the party to be raffled off. Attendees of the event buy tickets to try and win their favorite ornament. All those proceeds of the ticket sales go to our client, Greater Chicago Food Depository, to feed those in need in the Chicago area.
